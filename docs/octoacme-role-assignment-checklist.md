### Clarity Checks

For each assigned role, confirm the following before the project begins:

- [ ] The person in each role understands their **responsibilities** as defined in the Roles & Personas document.
- [ ] **Interaction points** between roles have been discussed (e.g., how the QA Lead will work with Developers on Definition of Done).
- [ ] Each role knows their **primary communication channels** and cadence (standups, syncs, reports).
- [ ] **Escalation paths** are clear — who does each role go to when blocked?
- [ ] Any **gaps or shared roles** (e.g., one person covering both BA and QA) have been acknowledged and risks noted.

### On-boarding Reminder

When a new team member joins mid-project:

- [ ] Share this checklist and the [Roles & Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) document.
- [ ] Introduce them to their counterparts in related roles.
- [ ] Confirm they have access to the project board, communication channels, and key artifacts.
